## The 8 Subgrades

TAG evaluates cards across 8 subgrade categories:

1. **Front Centering** - L/R and T/B positioning
2. **Back Centering** - L/R and T/B positioning
3. **Front Corners** - All 4 corners (fray, fill, sharpness)
4. **Back Corners** - All 4 corners
5. **Front Surface** - Scratches, scuffs, print defects
6. **Back Surface** - Wear, marks, defects
7. **Front Edges** - All 4 edges (fray, nicks, wear)
8. **Back Edges** - All 4 edges

---

## DINGS System

**DINGS** = Defects Identified of Notable Grade Significance

- DINGS are the PRIMARY reasons a card received its grade
- They carry the MOST WEIGHT in determining the final grade
- Not all DINGS are equal - severity matters
- A single severe DING can drop a grade more than multiple minor issues

### Key Principle: Scores COMPOUND, not average

> "A card will not grade significantly higher than its lowest subgrade score due to a prominent deviation"

Example: A card with 8.5-level corner wear will most likely grade 8.5 overall, regardless of other perfect subgrades.

## Grade Descriptions

### Pristine 10 (990-1000)
- "Virtually flawless" - exceeds industry standard
- Less than 1% of cards achieve this
- Front centering: 51/49 or better
- Back centering: 52/48 (TCG) or better
- Only Non-Human Observable Defects (NHODs) on surface

### Gem Mint 10 (950-989)
- Four sharp corners
- "Extremely attractive surface"
- Minimal edge wear
- Imperfections visible only under magnification
- Front: 55/45 or better
- Back: 65/35 (TCG) or better
- Equivalent to traditional 9.5

### Mint 9 (900-949)
- Sharp corners with up to 2 light touches
- Few small pits/scratches; no gloss disruption
- Minor print imperfections acceptable
- Surface wear on 1-2 edges acceptable

### NM-MT+ 8.5 (850-899) & NM-MT 8 (800-849)
- Sharp but touched corners with fraying
- Multiple surface effects (scuffing, print lines)
- Front: 62.5/37.5 (8.5) or 65/35 (8)

### NM+ 7.5 (750-799) & NM 7 (700-749)
- Worn, less sharp corners
- All four corners may fray
- Minor dents visible
- Front: 67.5/32.5 (7.5) or 70/30 (7)

### EX-MT+ 6.5 (650-699) & EX-MT 6 (600-649)
- 3+ corners show significant fraying
- Minor notches on edges
- Wrinkles/light stains possible
- Front: 72.5/27.5 (6.5) or 75/25 (6)

### EX+ 5.5 (550-599) & EX 5 (500-549)
- Larger surface wear areas
- Wrinkles, stains, scuffs, gloss loss
- Front: 77.5/22.5 (5.5) or 80/20 (5)

### VG-EX+ 4.5 (450-499) & VG-EX 4 (400-449)
- Rounded corners, possibly dirty
- Heavy wrinkles, bends, dings, creases
- Water damage possible
- Front: 82.5/17.5 (4.5) or 85/15 (4)

### VG+ 3.5 (350-399) & VG 3 (300-349)
- All corners rounded
- Severe scuffing, minor creases
- Text readability compromised
- Front: 87.5/12.5 (3.5) or 90/10 (3)

### Good+ 2.5 (250-299) & Good 2 (200-249)
- Heavy creases including potential full-length wrinkles
- Possible pinhole or glue (not both)
- Some text scuffed away
- One edge may have minor tear
- Front: 92.5/7.5 (2.5) or 95/5 (2)

### Fair 1.5 (150-199)
- Front centering: 98.33/1.67
- Falling corners
- Possible staple holes or tape residue (not both)

### Poor 1 (100-149)
- Multiple defects present
- Misshaped corners, excessive wear
- Tape or glue, larger tears, image obstructions

## Scoring Logic for Pre-Grader

### Centering-Only DING (like user's Mint 9 card)

If a card has ONLY a centering issue:
- Front centering at 56-60/40-44 = Mint 9 territory
- No corner/edge/surface DINGS = high base score
- Single category issue = grade limited by that category

### Recommended Scoring Formula

```
Base Score = 1000

For each DING:
  - CENTERING DING: Deduct based on how far past threshold
    - 56-60/44-40 front = -50 to -100 (still Mint 9 range)
    - 61-65/39-35 front = -100 to -150 (NM-MT 8-8.5 range)
    - 66-70/34-30 front = -150 to -250 (NM 7-7.5 range)

  - CORNER DING: Deduct based on severity
    - Light touch = -20 to -40
    - Visible wear = -50 to -100
    - Fraying = -100 to -200

  - EDGE DING: Deduct based on severity
    - Minor = -20 to -40
    - Moderate = -50 to -100
    - Significant = -100 to -200

  - SURFACE DING: Deduct based on severity
    - Minor (print line, tiny scratch) = -20 to -50
    - Moderate (scuff, visible scratch) = -50 to -150
    - Major (crease, dent, stain) = -150 to -400

Final Score = Base - sum(deductions)
Grade = lookup(Final Score)
```
