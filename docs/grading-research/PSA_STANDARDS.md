# PSA Card Grading Standards

## Overview
PSA (Professional Sports Authenticator) uses a 10-point scale. They DO use half-point grades (1.5, 2.5, etc.) but **NO PSA 9.5 exists**.

---

## Grade Scale & Names

| Grade | Official Name | Description |
|-------|---------------|-------------|
| **10** | Gem Mint | Essentially perfect - pristine corners, sharp focus, full original gloss, no imperfections |
| **9** | Mint | Nearly perfect with only one minor flaw |
| **8** | Near Mint-Mint (NM-MT) | Super high-end, may show very slight wax stain, slightest corner fraying, or minor print imperfection |
| **7** | Near Mint (NM) | Slight surface wear visible only upon close inspection |
| **6** | Excellent-Mint (EX-MT) | Visible surface wear or printing defect that doesn't detract from appeal |
| **5** | Excellent (EX) | Minor rounding of corners becoming evident, more visible surface wear |
| **4** | Very Good-Excellent (VG-EX) | Slightly rounded corners, evident surface wear, mild scratches |
| **3** | Very Good (VG) | Some rounding of corners (not extreme), evident surface wear |
| **2** | Good | Accelerated rounding on corners, obvious surface wear, may have chipping and creases |
| **1** | Poor | Major creasing, missing pieces, extreme discoloration, warping, destructive defects |

### Half-Point Grades
Available: **1.5, 2.5, 3.5, 4.5, 5.5, 6.5, 7.5, 8.5**

**IMPORTANT: There is NO PSA 9.5** - PSA felt a third "Mint" grade was unnecessary.

## Centering Requirements

**Updated Q1 2025**: PSA 10 front centering tightened from 60/40 to 55/45.

| Grade | Front Centering | Back Centering |
|-------|-----------------|----------------|
| **PSA 10** | 55/45 or better | 75/25 or better |
| **PSA 9** | 60/40 or better | 90/10 or better |
| **PSA 8** | 65/35 or better | 90/10 or better |
| **PSA 7** | 70/30 or better | 90/10 or better |
| **PSA 6** | 80/20 or better | 90/10 or better |
| **PSA 5** | 85/15 or better | 90/10 or better |
| **PSA 4** | 85/15 or better | 90/10 or better |
| **PSA 3** | 90/10 or better | 90/10 or better |
| **PSA 2** | 90/10 or better | 90/10 or better |
| **PSA 1** | 90/10 or better | 90/10 or better |

## Defect Tolerances

### Corner Wear by Grade
| Grade | Corner Condition |
|-------|------------------|
| 10 | Perfectly sharp, no wear or rounding |
| 9 | Very slight wear; essentially perfect |
| 8 | Slightest fraying at one or two corners |
| 6 | Corners may have slightly graduated fraying |
| 5 | Very minor rounding becoming evident |
| 3 | Some rounding (not extreme) |
| 2 | Accelerated rounding |
| 1 | Severe rounding and damage |

### Edge Wear by Grade
| Grade | Edge Condition |
|-------|----------------|
| 10 | Crisp and clean; flawless to naked eye |
| 9 | Very minor edge wear if any |
| 8 | Minimal edge wear acceptable |
| 7 | Minor fraying acceptable |
| 6 | Light edge wear acceptable |
| 5 | Minor chipping may be present |
| 3 | Noticeable edge wear |
| 1 | Severe edge damage and chipping |

### Surface by Grade
| Grade | Surface Condition |
|-------|-------------------|
| 10 | Perfect - no scratches, print lines, or gloss loss |
| 9 | One minor flaw acceptable (tiny scratch) |
| 8 | Very light scratches only under close inspection |
| 6 | Very light scratch may be detected upon close inspection |
| 5 | Several light scratches may be visible |
| 3 | Some apparent surface wear |
| 2 | More obvious surface wear |
| 1 | Extensive surface wear and damage |

## Special Labels & Qualifiers

| Label | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Authentic** | Genuine item, no numerical grade (alteration, major defect, or requested) |
| **Authentic Altered** | Genuine but altered (trimming, recoloring, restoration) |
| **MK** | Writing, ink marks, pencil marks present |
| **MC** | Atypical cut (miscut) |
| **OC** | Off-center (significant centering issues) |
| **OF** | Out of focus or out of register |
| **ST** | Staining visible |
| **PD** | Print defect (print lines, smudges) |

---

## Four Evaluation Criteria

1. **Centering** - Image placement on card
2. **Corners** - Sharpness and wear
3. **Edges** - Chipping, whitening, fraying
4. **Surface** - Scratches, gloss, print quality

---

## Key Notes

- **No subgrades displayed** - PSA gives single overall grade
- **2025 Update**: PSA 10 centering now requires 55/45 (was 60/40)
- **No 9.5 grade exists** - Jump from 9 to 10
- Half grades awarded when card is high-end for grade but not strong enough for next whole number
