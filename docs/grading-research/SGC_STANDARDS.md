# SGC Card Grading Standards

## Overview
SGC (Sportscard Guaranty Corporation) uses a 10-point scale with half-point increments. Transitioned from 100-point system in 2018. Known for being **stricter on back centering** than competitors.

## Two Types of Grade 10

### Pristine 10 (PRI)
- **Centering**: 50/50 perfect on both sides
- **Corners**: Four sharp, no visible wear
- **Surface**: No print/refractor lines, no visible wear under magnification
- **Label**: Gold trim
- **Rarity**: Very scarce, commands 5-7x premium over Gem Mint

### Gem Mint 10
- **Centering**: 55/45 front, 70/30 back
- **Corners**: Sharp, no visible wear
- **Surface**: Slight print spot allowable if aesthetically insignificant
- **Label**: Standard

## Defect Tolerances by Grade

### Grade 10 (Pristine)
- No print or refractor lines
- No visible wear under magnification
- Crisp focus
- Free of stains
- No breaks in surface gloss

### Grade 10 (Gem Mint)
- Clean and free from printing defects
- Sharp focus
- Slight print spot allowable if doesn't detract

### Grade 9-9.5
- Slight nick to one corner acceptable
- Small gloss breaks acceptable
- Minor surface scratches acceptable
- Minor print lines acceptable
- Small print spots acceptable

### Grade 8-8.5
- Corners sharp to naked eye, slight wear under magnification
- Small flaws acceptable (gloss breaks, scratches, print lines)

### Grade 7-7.5
- Slight wear on corners
- Minor scratching acceptable
- Print lines/refractor lines acceptable
- Some print spots acceptable

### Grade 6-6.5
- Slight fuzzing of corners
- Skewed cut more evident
- Slight notching of edges acceptable

### Grade 5
- Minor rounding/fuzzing of corners
- Roughness/chipping along edges (no layering)
- One slight surface crease may exist

### Grade 4 and below
- Increasingly heavy wear
- Creases, stains, rounded corners acceptable
- Structural damage increases

---

## Half-Point System

SGC uses half points from 1.5 to 9.5.

**Key Rule**: For a half-point grade, card must meet **centering requirements of the NEXT higher grade**.

Example: 8.5 requires 65/35 centering (same as 9's requirement).

---

## Special Labels

### Gold Label (Pristine 10)
- Visual gold trim on label
- Reserved for perfect 50/50 centering
- Significant premium over standard 10
- Very scarce designation

---

## Key Differences from Competitors

| Aspect | SGC | PSA | BGS |
|--------|-----|-----|-----|
| Back centering strictness | **Strictest** | Lenient | Moderate |
| Two 10 grades | Yes (Pristine/Gem) | No | Yes (Black/Gold) |
| Subgrades shown | No | No | Yes |
| Half points | Yes (1.5-9.5) | Yes (no 9.5) | Yes |
